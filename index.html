How it began

Our Love Story

We first met on a rainy Tuesday afternoon at a small bookshop in Paris, both reaching for the same worn copy of Hemingway. James smiled and said, "You have good taste." Sofia laughed and replied, "So do you — but I saw it first."

What followed was three hours of conversation over espresso, a shared umbrella back to the metro, and an exchange of numbers scribbled on a café receipt that Sofia still keeps in her journal.

Two years, twelve countries, and countless sunsets later, James got down on one knee at that same Parisian bookshop — holding a fresh copy of Hemingway, tucked inside of which was the original café receipt.

"She said yes before he finished the question."
